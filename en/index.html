About Us

We, the organizing team, decided in mid-2020 to start a Fridays For Future local group for Moosburg as well. After a six-month planning and organizing phase, we were ready at the beginning of 2021 to officially register as a local group and to plan and carry out our first campaigns. Our first campaign was a "spray chalk awareness campaign" in March 2021. Now we are ready and happy to announce the Global Strike on 24.09 also in Moosburg.
